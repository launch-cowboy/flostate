1. MECHANISM OF ACTION
================================================================================
Florencizumab (ONK-301) is a bispecific antibody targeting programmed death-ligand 1
(PD-L1) and vascular endothelial growth factor A (VEGF-A). The anti-PD-L1 arm restores
T-cell-mediated anti-tumor immunity by blocking PD-1/PD-L1 interaction. The anti-VEGF-A
arm inhibits tumor angiogenesis by neutralizing VEGF-A signaling through VEGFR-2.

Dual blockade is hypothesized to enhance anti-tumor efficacy through:
- Normalization of tumor vasculature → improved T-cell infiltration
- Reduction of VEGF-mediated immunosuppression in the tumor microenvironment
- Synergistic inhibition of tumor growth via complementary pathways

4. PHASE I DOSE ESCALATION (Study ONK-101)
================================================================================
Design:     3+3 dose escalation, 42 patients, 6 dose levels (0.3-20 mg/kg Q3W)
Population: Advanced solid tumors (NSCLC, melanoma, RCC, TNBC, bladder)
Results:
  DLT:      1 DLT at 20 mg/kg (Grade 3 immune-mediated hepatitis)
  MTD:      Not reached. Recommended Phase II Dose (RP2D): 10 mg/kg Q3W
  ORR:      19% (8/42) across all dose levels; 32% (6/19) at RP2D
  DCR:      62% at RP2D
  Median PFS: 4.8 months (95% CI: 3.2-7.1)

================================================================================
5. PHASE II EFFICACY (Study ONK-201)
================================================================================
Design:     Single-arm, 128 patients with PD-L1+ (TPS ≥ 1%) advanced NSCLC
Population: 2nd line after platinum-based chemotherapy
Results:
  ORR:      38.3% (49/128), 95% CI: 29.8%-47.3%
  CR:       4.7% (6/128)
  DCR:      71.1% (91/128)
  Median PFS: 7.2 months (95% CI: 5.8-9.1)
  Median OS: 14.8 months (95% CI: 11.5-18.2)
  Duration of Response: 11.3 months (95% CI: 8.2-NR)

Subgroup Analysis (PD-L1 ≥ 50%):
  ORR:      52.4% (22/42)
  Median PFS: 11.8 months

7. DRUG INTERACTIONS AND CONTRAINDICATIONS
================================================================================
Known Interactions:
- Systemic corticosteroids > 10 mg/day prednisone: May reduce efficacy of checkpoint
  inhibitor. Taper to ≤ 10 mg/day before initiating ONK-301.
- Live vaccines: Contraindicated during treatment and for 6 months after last dose.
- Other immunosuppressants: Avoid concurrent use (may blunt anti-tumor immune response).
- Anti-VEGF agents: Do not combine with other VEGF inhibitors (risk of additive toxicity).

Contraindications:
- Active autoimmune disease requiring systemic immunosuppressive treatment
- Prior organ transplant (risk of graft rejection)
- Active interstitial lung disease or history of Grade 3+ pneumonitis
- Known hypersensitivity to florencizumab or any excipient
- Pregnancy or breastfeeding
