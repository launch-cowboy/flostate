## 1. What you should know about this study
You are being asked to join a clinical research study. This study is testing an investigational drug, ONK-301, for advanced non-small cell lung cancer. 
*   **Version 3.0 Update:** This version includes updated safety information related to mild cardiac arrhythmias observed in recent trial phases. 

## 2. Risks and Discomforts
Like all medicines, the study drug can cause side effects. 
*   Fatigue
*   Nausea
*   *New (v3.0):* Potential for increased heart rate 

## 3. Financial Disclosure
You will not be charged for the study drug or any study-required procedures. You will be compensated for your time and travel at a rate of $75 per completed visit.
